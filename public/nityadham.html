Experience Unparalleled Luxury at Udupi

1 & 2 BHK Residential Apartments

Nithyadham is an exquisite residential complex envisioned and developed by Sai Radha Developers in the picturesque city of Udupi, Karnataka. With a strong focus on innovative design and home remodelling, Sai Radha Developers has meticulously crafted spacious affordable luxury apartments that cater to your every need, providing you with all the modern amenities you could ask for.

Located in the bustling heart of Udupi, Nithyadham boasts of top-class facilities that will make your life comfortable and secure. The building features an elegant entrance lobby that warmly welcomes you and your guests, and the automatic elevators make it easy for you to access your floor with ease. Nithyadham also provides a 24-hour generator backup to ensure you are never left in the dark during power outages.
