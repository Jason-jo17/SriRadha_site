About Us

Building Dreams, Creating Sweet Homes Since 1987.

Our Story

A Legacy of Trust and Quality

Established in 1987, Sai Radha Developers has been at the forefront of the real estate evolution in Udupi and Manipal. For over three decades, we have dedicated ourselves to transforming the skyline while ensuring the highest standards of living for our customers.

Under the visionary leadership of our Managing Director, Manohar S. Shetty, the company has successfully completed numerous prestigious projects. Our philosophy is simple: we don't just build apartments; we create 'Sweet Homes' where families can grow and thrive for generations.

Why Choose Us?

Modern Architecture & Premium Amenities

Every Sai Radha project is a blend of modern architectural brilliance and practical design. We select prime locations carefully to ensure convenience, connectivity, and tranquility for our residents.

From dedicated customer support to seamless post-handover services, we walk alongside our clients throughout their home-buying journey, ensuring absolute transparency and peace of mind.

Sai Radha Developers is synonymous with high-quality real estate projects, building 'Sweet Homes' in Udupi and Manipal since 1987.
