21 Acres Township

Discover your Personal Paradise in the Heart of Nature

3, 4 & 5 BHK Premium Villas

Sai Radha Township offers a meticulously planned community of luxury villas set amidst 21 sprawling acres of verdant landscapes in Manipal. This expansive development redefines resort-style living with independent homes that provide absolute privacy, expansive green spaces, and world-class communal facilities.

Whether you seek the tranquil environment for a morning walk along our dedicated jogging tracks, a refreshing dip in the swimming pool, or engaging activities at the modern clubhouse, Sai Radha Township perfectly balances the charm of nature with state-of-the-art urban conveniences. Experience community living that is safe, sustainable, and undeniably sophisticated.
